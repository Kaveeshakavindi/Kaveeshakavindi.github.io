Skin Disease App
The skin disease app is a revolutionary tool that utilizes image processing and artificial intelligence to accurately identify skin diseases and recommend appropriate treatments. Users can also find nearby doctors who specialize in the treatment of skin diseases, making it easier than ever to get the care they need.
Space Invaders
Space Invaders is a classic arcade game that has been recreated in Python using the Pygame library. The player controls a spaceship that shoots at alien invaders, and the goal is to defeat as many invaders as possible without being hit. The game features multiple levels, increasing difficulty, and different types of aliens with unique behaviors.
Hospital Management System
The Hospital Management System is a Java-based application that enables effective management of hospital-related tasks. It allows hospital administrators to keep track of patient records, manage staff, schedule appointments, and monitor the hospital's overall workflow. The system is designed to improve the efficiency and accuracy of hospital operations, resulting in better patient care and satisfaction.
